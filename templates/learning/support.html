Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to create a new password. The link will expire in 24 hours for security reasons.

Why can't I access my course materials?

This could happen for several reasons:

  • Your subscription may have expired
  • There might be a temporary system issue
  • Your browser cache needs to be cleared

Try refreshing the page or logging out and back in. If the problem persists, contact our support team.

How does the AI personalization work?

Our AI analyzes your learning patterns, quiz results, and engagement metrics to:

  • Adjust the difficulty of materials
  • Recommend relevant content
  • Identify your strengths and weaknesses
  • Personalize your learning path

The more you use the platform, the better it adapts to your needs.

How do I contact my instructor?

You can contact instructors through:

  1. The course discussion forum
  2. Direct messaging in your dashboard
  3. Scheduled office hours (see course details)

Most instructors respond within 24-48 hours.
